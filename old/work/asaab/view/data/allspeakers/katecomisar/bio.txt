<p>
    Katie was a member of the 1999 Lakota West State Championship soccer team.
    She was a four-year letter winner. Katie won the Coach's Award her senior
    year.
</p>
<p>
    Katie went on to play soccer at Wright State University. She represented
    Wright State at the Horizon League Leadership Council. She graduated with a
    degree in Middle Childhood Education (math and science). Kate was also a
    member of the Kappa Delta Pi Education Honor Society.
</p>
<p>
    Kate earned her Master of Education as an Intervention Specialist (Mild to
    Moderate) from Wright State University in 2009.
</p>
<p>
    Katie is currently an Intervention Specialist and coaches JV soccer at
    Mason High School.
</p>
