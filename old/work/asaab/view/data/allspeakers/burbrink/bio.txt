Kat Burbrink was a freshman on the 2011 Final Four Team. She was a 2x Second Team All-GMC selection and was also named 1st Team.  She ranks 9th on the Lakota West record board with 59 career RBIs.
Kat went on to play at Trevecca Nazarene University where she played in 200 games in her career.  She is now an assistant coach at the university.
