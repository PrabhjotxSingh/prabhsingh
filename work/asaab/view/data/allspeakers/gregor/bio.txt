
Lakota Class of 1992: Basketball

<p>
    A 1992 graduate, he was a three-year letterman in basketball, was named
    player of the year in Cincinnati in 1992 and was named First Team
    All-State. He was team captain his last two years, when the Thunderbirds
    made it to the state basketball finals in 1991 and won the state tournament
    in 1992. Keith was in the inaugural Lakota Hall of Fame class.
</p>
<p>
    Keith went on to play at the University of Cincinnati where he was a 3 year
    captain.
</p>
<p>
    Keith is the US Clinical Sales Manager at Hansen Medical
</p>
