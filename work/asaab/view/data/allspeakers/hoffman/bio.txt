Amanda Lahti Hoffman was a junior on the 2011 Final Four team.  She was a 3x All-GMC selection being named 2nd team in 2010 and 1st team in 2011 & 2012.  Amanda ranks  9th on the record board with a .354 career batting average.  
Amanda went on to play at Ohio Northern University where she was named 2nd Team All-Conference in 2014.
Coach Lahti is now a member of the Lakota West Softball Staff.
